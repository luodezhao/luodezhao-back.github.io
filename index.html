Jiabo (Raymond) Huang is currently a PhD candidate within the Computer Vision Group in the School of Electronic Engineering and Computer Science at Queen Mary University of London, supervised by Prof. Shaogang (Sean) Gong.

Before that, he worked closely with Prof. Wei-Shi Zheng and Research Prof. Xiao-Hua Xie within the Intelligence Science & System Lab (ISEE). He was awarded B.Eng and M.Eng by Sun Yat-sen University in 2016 and 2018, respectively.

His current research is in Computer Vision and Machine Learning, with a focus on deep learning with insufficient human supervision, e.g. unsupervised and transfer learning.
